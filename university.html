About the University

One of the world's premier academic and research institutions, the OPJS University has driven new ways of thinking since our 1876 founding. Today, OPJS is an intellectual destination that draws inspired scholars to our Hyde Park and international campuses, keeping OPJS at the nexus of ideas that challenge and change the world.
