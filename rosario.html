About Us

Our Story

Cocoon is driven by uncompromising freshness, quality, and courteous hospitality. Founded in 2020 by trio Rajeev, Neeraj and Gaurav, 'Cocoon' began their journey with an idea of serving mouth-watering authentic Indian dishes with kids’ friendly environment. Mr. Rajeev, an IITian, Mr. Neeraj, a Doctor and Mr. Gaurav, an Engineer are divided by profession but are real foodies at heart. Being travelled nationally and internationally, we analyzed the gap between the hygiene standards followed in India and the rest of the world. Therefore, we sealed the gap by implementing the internationally followed hygiene standards at the restaurant.

At Cocoon, we are fully committed to cooking and serving fresh and quality food - with signature choices like our (chef's special). We're grateful that our restaurant has passionate employees who delight every guest with an exceptional dining experience. From our ongoing efforts to bring you the best menu to the gracious smiles and genuine hospitality, Cocoon wants to make our authentic Indian Dining your favourite dining choice.

Our Food Philosophy



At Cocoon, we are passionate about food. We believe "Happy Parent- Happy Kids"; therefore, we have provided ample space to let your kids go free. We have dedicated a corner for kids to play and interact with each other while parents enjoy their meal. Why should adults have all the fun? Patrons also know us for our child-friendly staff and a kid-friendly menu. We take great caution to make sure we serve safe and delicious food with excellent quality.

Our Vision



Our vision is an uncompromising commitment to providing you with the best dining experience. We take the utmost care to ensure that we serve authentic food with quality ingredients and develop a menu that fits everyone.
We believe "Happy Parent- Happy Kids," hence the play station. The concept was to have an entertaining space for kids and adults, so while their parents appreciate a peaceful feast, the kids can have a fun time too. The dining area is next to the kids' zone, allowing the parents to monitor their kids.
We are focused on bringing pleasant experience by serving delicious meals and maintaining international hygienic standards. We are committed to bringing excitement and joy to our customers' lives by providing a delightful variety of meals on the table. We believe a deep desire to serve others is the secret element of a cordial host. And that's why we want you to always leave with a smile.
