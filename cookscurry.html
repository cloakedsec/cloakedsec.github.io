Our Story

Founded in 2020 by trio Rajeev, Neeraj and Gaurav 'Cooks and Curries' began their journey with the idea to serve mouth-watering authentic Indian dishes in a convenient, environment friendly and good quality packing. Mr. Rajeev, an IITian, Mr. Neeraj, a Doctor and Mr. Gaurav, an Engineer, are divided by profession but are real foodies at heart. Being traveled nationally and internationally, they analyzed the gap between the hygiene standards followed in India and the rest of the world. Therefore, they decided to seal the gap by implementing the internationally followed hygiene standards at Cooks and Curries.
